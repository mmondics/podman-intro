Park Closures

  • Old Carriage Trail Bridge

    Alert 1, Severity closure, Old Carriage Trail Bridge
    Approximately .3 miles from the northern trail entrance, the bridge is closed due to severe erosion of the bank underneath. No detour available.

  • Modified Train Operations

    Date Posted: 10/13/2022Alert 2, Severity closure, Modified Train Operations
    The National Park Service has closed portions of the tracks used by Cuyahoga Valley Scenic Railroad due to significant erosion from the Cuyahoga River. Limited train excursions will be provided. Visit cvsr.org for more information.
    more

  • Brandywine Golf Course Property

    Alert 3, Severity closure, Brandywine Golf Course Property
    The former golf course property, including a section of the Valley Trail, is closed during environmental remediation.
    more

Danger

  • Brandywine Falls Safety Alert

    Alert 1, Severity danger, Brandywine Falls Safety Alert
    Do not climb the rocks, over the fenced areas, or Brandywine Falls. There is a 50 ft closure at both the top and bottom of the falls. Entering closed areas could result in serious injury or death. The viewing deck and Gorge Trail is open and accessible.

A River Renewed

Though a short distance from the urban areas of Cleveland and Akron, Cuyahoga Valley National Park seems worlds away. The park is a refuge for native plants and wildlife, and provides routes of discovery for visitors. The winding Cuyahoga River gives way to deep forests, rolling hills, and open farmlands. Walk or ride the Towpath Trail to follow the historic route of the Ohio & Erie Canal.
